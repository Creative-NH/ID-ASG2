CCAs

Here are some of our available CCAs.


Photography

Fancy yourself a photographer? Join the Photography Club and you'll be introduced to photography basics as well as the technical know-how of photography and videography, including the appreciation of aesthetics.

Contact us at cca_photography@connect.np.edu.sg


Softball

With a strong emphasis on extensive teamwork, cooperation and communication between members, softball is ideal for those keen on trying out a new and challenging sport.

Contact us at: cca_softball@connect.np.edu.sg


BB Primers

Established under the umbrella of The Boys' Brigade in Singapore, NP BB Primers are given numerous opportunities to develop and nurture their leadership abilities and character, from event planning to taking part in the National Day Parade (NDP) as crowd motivators

Contact us at cca_primers@connect.np.edu.sg


Astronomy Club

At the Astronomy Club, our passion can't be contained solely within NP! With growing interest we see in the sciences, we have also imparted our knowledge on astronomy to members of the public. Come be part of our club and discover the wonders of our galaxy and beyond.

Contact us at cca_astronomy@connect.np.edu.sg
